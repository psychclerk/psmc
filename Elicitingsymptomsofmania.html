Eliciting symptoms of mania


  • Mood
    • 'Of late how has your mood been?’                        
    • ‘How long has it been that way?’                        
    • ‘Are you staying more happy than usual?’
    • ‘Do you get irritated more often than usual?’                        
  • Energy        
    • ‘How have your energy levels been?’
    • ‘How long has that been?’                        
  • Interest
    • Asks how the patient has been using their increased energy levels? [increased goal directed activity]
      • ‘How have you been spending your new-found energy?’
      • ‘Like…. have you been…’
      • Work - ‘Working more?’, ‘Taking up new projects or responsibilities?’ 
      • Social - ‘Talking to a lot of people?’
      • Psychomotor - ‘Feeling restless or agitated?'                        
  • Sleep
    • 'How is your sleep?'                        
    • ‘How many hours of sleep have you been getting?’
    • ‘Do you feel rested with that many hours of sleep?’                        
  • Concentration        
    • Asks the patient about change in ability to concentrate?
      • ‘How has your ability to focus been recently?’
      • ‘… can you focus long enough so that you could finish your tasks… like listening to a song or watching s television episode?’        
  • Pace of thoughts
    • ‘Have you been more talkative than usual?’                        
    • 'Have you been experiencing that your thoughts are going very fast?’                        
    • 'Do you feel that your thoughts are jumping from one to another?’                        
  • Self-worth
    • ‘Do you feel you are different than other people?..In what ways?’                        
  • Psychotic symptoms
    • ‘Do you feel you have special power or ability?
    • ‘Do you feel that your life has a special purpose?
    • ‘Do you feel you have a special relationship with a celebrity or god?
    • ‘Do you feel people talking about you?
    • ‘Do you feel people are trying to harm you?’
    • Any other if required                        
    • ‘Have you told about these to others?’
    • ‘And what has been their reactions? Do they believe you?’
    • Or ‘Do you feel these thoughts are 100% true?’                        
    • ‘Have you been having odd experiences like hearing voices that others cannot hear?’
    • ‘Any other odd experiences that cannot be easily explained?’                        
  • Risks        
    • Screening - ‘Since the time that you have been feeling [MOOD], have you done something that could cause trouble for you or your family?’ … Like ….
    • Spending – ‘Have you been spending a lot?’
    • Substance – ‘Have you used alcohol or recreational drugs?’
    • Sexual – ‘Has there been a change in your sexual desire?’
    • Suicidal – ‘Have you had thoughts of hurting yourself?’
    • Homicidal – ‘Do you have a lot of anger or frustration? Have you thought of hurting someone?


  • Collateral history from a reliable informant.
